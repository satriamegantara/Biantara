What Drives Us

Biantara was established to participate in the Indonesian Flying Robot Contest (KRTI). We are a team of students from Universitas Jenderal Soedirman who share a strong passion for unmanned aerial technology and autonomous systems.

Driven by a spirit of innovation and a deep sense of dedication, our team is committed to developing UAV technology that contributes positively to society and supports technological advancement in Indonesia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Biantara UAV Unsoed carries out its core research, development, and flight-testing activities at the Faculty of Engineering, Universitas Jenderal Soedirman in Purbalingga, Central Java.

Biantara consists of several subdivisions:

  • Mechanic Subdivision
  • Electronics Subdivision
  • System Subdivision
  • Manager Subdivision

Each subdivision in Biantara UAV Unsoed plays a unique role in supporting the organization's mission:

  • Mechanic Subdivision focuses on the design, manufacture, and maintenance of UAV structures and airframes.
  • Electronics Subdivision is responsible for integrating sensors, power systems, wire, and electronic components.
  • System Subdivision handles software development, flight control systems, and autonomous navigation.
  • Manager Subdivision manages administrative tasks, public relations, and funding.

We have developed several UAV prototypes for competitions, including racing planes and fixed-wing aircraft. Our current fleet are named PLR-003 which is the abbreviation of Pesawat Latih Racing.
